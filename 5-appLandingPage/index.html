Why Use This App???

This app is designed to help those who would prefer to be curled up on their couch with a good book to get outdoors and exercise. There are a variety of exercise options to pass each hurtle and all must be completed to journey over middle earth and defeat the armies of Mordor.

Strike Down the Balrog!

You are a servant of the Secret Fire, wielder of the flame of Anor. Energize yourself with some hot yoga, a long walk, and a short sprint. Then say to that Balrog, "You shall not pass!"

They're taking the Hobbits to Isengard!

HURRY! The orcs have carried Mary and Pippen off toward Isengard! Run, speed-walk, or bike your way along their trail. The faster to go the sooner you'll be able to rescue the original dynamic duo.

The Ents Go to War!

Wage war on Isengard as an Ent! Defeate Isengad and revenge the trees that were sacrificed to its forges by lifting, climbing, and hiking.

Battle Shelob!

Take on Shelob to defend Frod and the Ring! Use Sam's natural strengths to save the day. Go for a long walk, dance a little and do some gardening!

The Last Battle for Middle Earth!

Lead the armies of Middle Earth agaist the armies of Mordor! Draw the Eye of Sauron away from Mount Doom! Get your entire fellowship together for a group outing. Go kayaking, swimming, hiking, climbing. Whatever it takes to defeat the forces of evil!
